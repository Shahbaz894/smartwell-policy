Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 18, 2026 | Last Updated: May 18, 2026

SmartWell System ("we", "our", or "us") is dedicated to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how our mobile application collects, uses, manages, and safeguards device information, telemetry data, and operational logs to deliver robust hardware monitoring solutions.

1. Information We Collect

To operate schedules, manage active runtime logs, and track performance indicators, our software uses the following data vectors:

  • Hardware Identifiers: We collect and bind your specific device_id or unique hardware serial identifiers to authenticate your smartphone with your designated microcontrollers.
  • Telemetry Data Logs: We monitor, display, and record real-time operational parameters transmitted from your hardware modules via MQTT brokers, including motor voltage, system current levels, reference frequency markers, and rotational speeds.
  • User-Supplied Metatags: Optional operational strings provided by you, such as customer_name labels or custom schedule aliases, to organize report listings.

2. How We Use Data

Collected variables are processed solely to facilitate core app behavior, specifically:

  • To dispatch target execution triggers ("ON" / "OFF") to microcontrollers securely.
  • To construct internal countdown loops and track running duration blocks.
  • To parse telemetry strings into human-readable data visualization charts within the mobile interface.

3. Data Storage & Retention

Operational data structures, parameters, and custom labels are securely synchronized via cloud-hosted backends (utilizing structured relational databases and encrypted storage environments). Data records remain accessible until requested for deletion by the associated user.

4. Third-Party Data Transmission

We do not sell, rent, monetize, or distribute hardware serial variables or customer metadata to third-party ad networks or brokers. Data strings are routed exclusively through our designated, secure MQTT message brokers to facilitate direct end-to-end communication with your hardware controllers.

5. Google Play Store Policy Compliance

In accordance with Google Play Developer Policies regarding hardware and system integrations, we explicitly declare that this application does not track coarse location targets in the background, read internal device contact databases, or access file system storage layers without permission context windows.
